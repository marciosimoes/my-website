Rebrand of the Show da Física project

During my work at Quanta Júnior, a Junior Enterprise of the Physics and Mathematics course at Unicamp, I carried out the rebrand of the physics show project, a product of the company that aimed to bring a dynamic and fun presentation of physics experiments to schools.

All the fundamental graphic materials were built to support the use of the Brand, in addition t-shirt models and a new website following the new brand.

Technologies used: Adobe Photoshop, CorelDRAW, Wordpress, PHP, MySQL.
