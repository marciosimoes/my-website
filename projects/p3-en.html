Creation of an internal Social Network for the School

The ETEC Social project was developed in 2014 as a Final Project by ETEC Pedro Ferreira Alves and was presented at the "FETEPS 2016" and "FATEC de Portas Abertas" fairs, being one of the best projects of the year.

Aiming to create a common environment for interaction between students and teachers, the system had a series of tools and functionalities that help the student throughout the school year, encouraging him to learn inside and outside the school, through interactive and innovative resources.

Technologies used: PHP 7, Laravel, Materialize-CSS, jQuery, HTML5, CSS3, SQL, MySQL.
